## THE 8-DOC CAMPAIGN BOOK PREVIEW

**NIGEL:**
> "Now — what comes *after* this workshop.
>
> Abracadabra has a canonical post-foundation deliverable stack — the 8-doc Campaign Book. Every new client or new campaign at this agency produces this. You'll find the spec at `07_Admin/_System/NEW_CLIENT_CAMPAIGN_STACK.md` if you want to walk through it later. For now: the cascade.
>
> The Campaign Book is what *operationalizes* what we locked here. It's not in scope this week — but Ishan walks into Kinder's saying *here's the foundation, AND here's the next 6-8 weeks of work that turns it into a launchable plan.* That's the roadmap signal."

### The 8 docs (in cascade order)

| # | Doc | What it answers | Owner | Pressure-test |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Strategy Brief** | What's the business problem, marketing problem, comms problem? Strategic insight + territory. Role of communications. | Julian Cole | Steel feeds audience inputs |
| 2 | **Audience Brief** | Who exactly are we talking to and what's the human truth they can't say out loud that we can unlock? | Jon Steel | Julian for fit to Strategy Brief |
| 3 | **Creative Brief** | One single-minded proposition. What we want them to think, feel, do. Tone. Mandatories. | Julian Cole | Hegarty (bravery) + McCabe (copy) + Fink (visual) |
| 4 | **Comms Brief** | What role does each channel play in the consumer journey? Strategic comms architecture (not media buying). | Julian Cole | Depesh + Chase for channel-feasibility |
| 5 | **Channel Plan** | Operational media buy. Budget, placements, optimization criteria, learning agenda. | Depesh Mandalia (paid) / Chase Dimond (owned) | Julian for fit to Comms Brief |
| 6 | **Hook Compliance Grid** (or per-vertical compliance gate) | No-go / grey / green opening visuals + lines. For 4 All Snacks: FDA structure/function compliance + kids' advertising compliance | Saul + Indigo | Julian + Hegarty |
| 7 | **Production Brief** | What we actually make: concept slate, register variety, execution standards, schedule, format spec. | Indigo Sato | Hegarty + McCabe + Fink upstream; Massimo for packaging system |
| 8 | **Measurement Framework** | What does success look like? Volume / quickness / quality-of-craft / case-quality / learning agenda. KPIs. | Sharp + Julian | Nigel for commercial fit |

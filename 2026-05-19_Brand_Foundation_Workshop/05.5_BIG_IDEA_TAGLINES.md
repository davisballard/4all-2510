## CONSTRAINTS (LOCKED — every line must clear all of these)

- **No "kids" in the line.** You don't say *kids* to a kid.
- **No "pop" anchoring** (soda-pop or otter-pop). Format-specific. Brand is a platform across freeze pop / fruit snack / chip / cereal.
- **No sexual double reads** (no *G-spot, "big X energy"→big dick energy*, etc.). Dad-eye-roll-survivable.
- **No fuel / health-food / nutrition-claim language.** It's a sugar snack 10% better, not a fueling-the-day product. Saul gates all literal claims.
- **No comparison.** No *"unlike X / better than X / wellness left."* Positive-POV only. Speak from our own block.
- **Kid-first.** A 5-year-old can quote it. A 9-year-old says it on the playground.
- **Platform-scalable.** Works for freeze pop AND fruit snack AND chip AND cereal — not just SKU-1.
- **Authentic to product behavior.** Cold · color · squeeze · crunch · share · grab · pick. Not *fuel · day · running.*

## Guardrails (all transcript-stated)

- **Typeface / font selection = Joe's domain.** Don't burn time on it. *"To me those are the areas I would say, don't spend a lot of time on that... Joe can also have more [input]."* — Elliot. Reference pulls only if anything.
- **Color blocks delivered AI-ready.** So Elliot can drop into image gen for downstream variation.
- **Mood boards = market pulls, not generative.** Verbatim guardrail.
- **Awareness > conversion** for this launch. *"It's literally just getting the logo in front of them. So when they see it on the shelves, they go I know that."* — Davis. *"89% of awareness... is literally just like what Kinder's done well — I saw that, I already had that logo in my head."* — Davis. Awareness may matter more for one persona than another — *"potentially for one [persona] and not another."* — Elliot.
- **Channel strategy = tactics, not execution.** No campaign creative, no week-by-week post calendar, no big-creative event design.
- **Brand book ≠ mockup.** Founder / his designer owns mockup execution.
- **Meeting modality = conversation, not slides.**
- **Ishan stays the driving force on the Joe relationship.** *"He's not pulling in other people. The more we're able to iron down with him... you're the money, you're the relationship. But we've got this under control."* — Elliot. Deliverable shape supports Ishan running the meeting solo with the package.
